== Description ==

Automate your blog in 3 steps and increase your traffic!
Save time and resources by allowing our plugin to automate your blog. The plugin will periodically create unique, high-quality SEO-optimized articles specifically tailored to your site's content and subject matter.

This can increase your traffic by as much as 300% after just the first month of the plugin!
 
# Generate unique articles
Use the power of automatic generation of unique, SEO-optimized articles to enrich the content on your blog and attract more readers. 
Before each publication, you have the ability to review and edit articles to stay in full control!

# Only 3 easy steps!
After logging in and going through just 3 simple steps, you can fully automate the blog creation process
1. Determine the theme of the site
This will ensure that the articles are perfectly tailored to your site.

2. Choose the language and author of the posts
Specify what author should be signed in the articles and the language in which the entries should be created.

3. Choose the timing of recurring publications
Decide on which days of the week and at what time you want to publish articles. 
Remember that you can review each one before publishing.

# Use AI technology to create content
The WordPress plugin from Boostsite is the perfect solution for those who want to automate the process of creating and publishing content to their blog.

By intelligently analyzing subject matter and existing content based on the GPT-4 model, the plugin generates and publishes unique, high-quality, SEO-optimized articles - plus there's the ability to review and edit articles before publishing, which gives you additional control.

With WP AutoPost AI, creating a blog becomes simple and efficient, giving you more time for other aspects of your business.
